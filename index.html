We do things differently. We’ve partnered with dozens of departments, and delivery teams across government to design and implement high-impact programs that work in the real world.

Using outcomes-based financing, sludge audits, and a variety of other data-informed methods, we help teams deliver meaningful results. We cut time, cost and complexity.

Why Impact Canada is able to drive real change in government and get results.

  • A specialized team in Privy Council Office (PCO) with expertise in advanced data analytics, social and behavioural sciences, and partnership brokering.
  • Bespoke data collection and rigorous methodologies developed to work in a Canadian context
  • Connected to partners via embedded staff for maximum reach and impact.
  • Mandated to drive advanced policy analysis on the government's top priorities.
  • Designed to scale without increasing the impact on the fiscal framework.
